If you are interested in reading further on the subject of mushrooms and mushroom cultivation, here are some book recommendations:

Entangled Life by Merlin Sheldrake is one of the most captivating books I have ever read. It gives a good overview on the hidden life of mushrooms all around us. There also is an Audiobook, of which you can get a small teaser at this link, that is VERY enjoyable. If you are interested in how these fascinating organisms work, you should give this a read.

Radical Mycology by Peter Mccoy is a very in depth book about all kinds of mushrooms, this is probably the most comprehensive book you will find on general mushroom growing. The amount of knowledge in this book is insane and this will give you A LOT to work with on your journey, if you are able to get your hands on a copy. It contains everything from general mushroom biology to cultivation methods for all kinds of different species.
